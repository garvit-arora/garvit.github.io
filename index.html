Important Updates

National Immunisation Day

The next National Immunisation Day for Polio vaccination is scheduled for April 10, 2025. Ensure all children under 5 years receive the oral polio vaccine.

New Pneumococcal Vaccine

The indigenous Pneumococcal Conjugate Vaccine has been added to the Universal Immunisation Programme, protecting children against pneumonia.

Mobile Vaccination Units

Mobile vaccination units are now serving remote areas across 18 states, bringing immunisation services closer to underserved communities.

Digital Vaccination Records

Access your family's vaccination records through the CoWIN platform or the UMANG app. Digital certificates are now available for all vaccines.

Ayurveda & Holistic Wellness

Embrace the ancient wisdom of Ayurveda fused with modern technology. Our platform redefines wellness by integrating AI-driven diagnostics, immersive experiences, and personalized e-commerce—all centered around traditional Ayurvedic principles.

Naadi Pariksha 2.0: Advanced Digital Diagnostics

Reimagining the ancient art of Naadi Pariksha, our system integrates sensor data and real-time analytics to provide evidence-based health reports. Experience enhanced biohacking innovations and secure health data management like never before.

X-Verse Ayurveda Experience

Step into an immersive 3D “X-Verse” environment that blends an Apple-like UI/UX with interactive visualizations of your wellness journey. Explore dynamic simulations that illustrate the synergy between traditional Ayurvedic practices and modern diagnostic tools.

Ayurvedic E-Shop & Lifestyle Guidance

Discover personalized product recommendations—from herbal supplements to organic skincare—tailored to your unique constitution. Our e-commerce interface also offers lifestyle and dietary guidance to help you achieve balanced health.

AI-Powered Ayurveda Consultations

Book consultations with experienced Ayurvedic practitioners or engage with our conversational AI for real-time health advice. Receive continuously updated insights, ensuring that your wellness strategy is always evidence-based and personalized.

Future-Proof Ayurveda Ecosystem

Our platform is built to evolve. With a scalable ecosystem roadmap, community-driven insights, and optional IoT integration, we’re redefining what it means to achieve long-term holistic wellness.

Ayurvedic Immunity Boosters & Remedies

Complement your vaccination and modern treatments with natural Ayurvedic remedies:

  • Ashwagandha: Boosts immunity and reduces stress.
  • Tulsi (Holy Basil): Known for its antiviral and antibacterial properties.
  • Giloy: Supports detoxification and enhances natural defense mechanisms.
  • Triphala: A traditional formulation that aids in digestion and overall health.
  • Neem: Promotes skin health and offers natural antibacterial benefits.

These time-honoured remedies can act as complementary measures alongside modern immunisation strategies to enhance overall wellness.
